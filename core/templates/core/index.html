Welcome to Strategyx


Strategyx is a powerful CRM (Customer Relationship Management) platform that helps businesses streamline their customer interactions and drive growth. Our innovative solution provides a centralized hub for managing leads, contacts, sales, and customer support.

The problem we solve

Fragmented Customer Data, Businesses often struggle with customer information spread across multiple systems, making it difficult to get a comprehensive view of each customer.

Our Unique solution

Through a centralised platform, Strategyx consolidates all customer data and interactions into a single, intuitive interface, providing a 360-degree view of each customer.

Work in a Team? Great! The platform also allows management of teams, team members and asigning customer's to a team member to foster clear communication and ensure clarity.
